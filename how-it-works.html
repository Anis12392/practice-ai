The Problem

You have things to say but you're stuck between thinking and formatting.

You try to think AND write a tweet at the same time. Result: nothing gets posted.

Dump.ai separates the two. You think. We format.

How It Works

1

Setup your brand (once)

Who you are, your goals, your tone, examples of posts you like. The AI learns YOUR voice.

|
2

Dump your thoughts

Talk, type, send a photo, or a voice memo. No structure needed. Just dump what happened today.

|
3

Get posts

3 tweet options + 1 LinkedIn post. In YOUR tone. Aligned with YOUR goals. Ready to publish.
